ABOUT US

Mystic Treasure began with a simple idea: to design fine jewelry that gives people a sense of uniqueness. All of it started in a small workshop where a few friends who loved making one-of-a-kind accessories got together. They put a lot of effort and talent into making each piece gleam elegantly.

An increasing number of people were fascinated with Mystic Treasure's jewelry as word of their works spread. These days, Mystic Treasure is a well-liked company, recognized for its classic items that introduce any ensemble with a hint of magic. We keep making imaginative jewelry that inspires a sense of luxury in every user.

Myles Casanas

Hi, I'm Myles Casanas, the proud owner and founder of Mystic Treasure. Ever since I can remember, I've been passionate about all kinds of art.

This lifelong love for creativity and self-expression inspired me to combine my artistic vision with my appreciation for durable, timeless materials. That's how the concept of metal art stones, gold and unique accessories was born. Each piece I design and craft reflects my dedication to merging beauty with functionality.

My mission is to transform everyday objects into extraordinary works of art that bring joy and inspiration to your life. Thank you for being a part of this artistic journey. I hope my creations inspire you as much as they have inspired me to bring them to life.
